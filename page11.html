About The Tour

Get a taste for the indigenous culture and amazing scenery of Ecuador during this 10-hour trip covering variety of cultural and natural sights. Stop at a viewpoint with views of Cayambe and Cotopaxi, then check out the ‘Mitad del Mundo’ monument located directly on the earth’s equator.

You’ll also explore Cuicocha Crater Lake, visit a local biscuit factory and go shopping at the indigenous markets at Otavalo and Cotacachi. This Ecuador sightseeing trip also includes lunch, a guide and round-trip transport from Quito.

1

Quitsato Sundial located at the Equator line

This tour begins with a scenic drive north of Quito through the Sierra highlands. Our first stop will be at Quitsato Sundial located at the Equator line, where you can balance on the equator! 

2

Visit at the Cayambe bizcochos (local biscuits) factory

Then we’ll continue to Cayambe where we can taste and learn about the town’s famous bizcochos (local biscuits), here you can also have breakfast.

3

 Viewpoint of San Pablo Lake

San Pablo Lake is one more of the beautiful areas in the province of Imbabura, 4 km west of the town of Otavalo and 2670 meters over sea level. "La Laguna de San Pablo" is an ecosystem that includes some representative species such as herons, ducks, some amphibians and many aquatic plants. It is also found at the foot of Imbabura volcano, from which flows the water discharged by the waterfall Peguche.

4

Indigenous market of Otavalo

From that point, we’ll continue to the famous market of Otavalo where you’ll have the option to purchase local handicrafts. The main market day in Otavalo is Saturday; however, this tour can be operated throughout the week (Tuesdays). Our professional guide will provide you an explanation of the indigenous people of this area, such as the Quitu, Cara and other pre-Inca groups, and some extra information of the city

5

Leather shops in Cotacachi

Our next stop will be Cotacachi, a town famous for its leather garments. Before strolling through this town we receive lunch in a local restaurant. 

6

Laguna de Cuicocha

Afterwards, we’ll go to the beautiful Laguna Cuicocha, which is located in a volcano crater and contains 2 green islands.
After spend an unforgettable time in this precious landscape, we return to Quito.
