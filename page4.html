How does it work?

Daily navigable excursions to different uninhabited islands in the archipelago. The programs are 4, 5 and 6 days. Navigations to these islands are done in mini motor yachts.

The tour is based in Isla de Santa Cruz (Puerto Ayora). From Puerto Ayora, you will embark on small motor yacht with different itinearies: Bartolomé, Plazas, Seymour Norte and / or Santa Fe.
You can know at least two of these islands (4-day tour) and up to 3 islands (6-day tour).
