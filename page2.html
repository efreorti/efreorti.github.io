Is it good for me?


The Navigable Itineraries are a type of tour in Galapagos based on the Island of Santa Cruz (Puerto Ayora). From Puerto Ayora, you will embark on small motor yacht with  different itineraries: Bartolomé, Plazas, Seymour Norte and / or Santa Fe.

If you enjoy good physical conditions, this tour is for you. If you like more comfort, we recommend our Galapagos Tours on board cruise.

What is included?

• Complete menu (type food).
• Accommodation.
• Transfer in / out at the Galapagos airport.
• Daily asisstance and support.
• Land and maritime transport in shared.
• Visits and shared excursions according to itinerary.
 Charles Darwin Station.
 Tortuga Bay Beach.
 Upper Part (Private Ranch and Lava Tunnels).
 Garrapatero beach.
 Port Tour.
 Full Day Navigable.
• Guides authorized by the Galapagos National Park.
• Water and soft drinks (not bottled) during meals.
• Taxes.
