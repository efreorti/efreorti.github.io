From "Puerto Ayora" 


One day Tours from Puerto Ayora on the island of Santa Cruz have 4 destinations aboard motor yachts:
• North Seymour Island

One Day Tours from Puerto Villamil on Isabela Island include 3 places for snorkeling and hiking:
• Cabo Rosa also known as «Los Tuneles»

• Hike to Sierra Negra Volcano

From "Puerto Baquerizo"

One day tours from Puerto Baquerizo on San Cristobal Island include two of the most beautiful places in Galapagos:
• Tour to Islote León Dormido (Kicker Rock) - one of the most biodiverse sites in Galapagos
• Tour around the Spanish island - paradise of the birds in Galapagos
